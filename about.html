About Kaashy

Kaashy is a fiat swap startup that simplifies digital finance by unifying mobile money and bank wallets in one app. Kaashy also includes its own secure in-app wallet where users can easily save, send, and receive money.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

Our mission is to make digital finance in frontier markets more connected, more accessible, and more user-focused.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become the leading digital finance platform, giving everyone access to simple, secure, and connected financial services.
